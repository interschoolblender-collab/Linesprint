About LineSprint' 25

A simple robotics competition where students in Classes 6-8 build autonomous robots to follow a black line on a white surface. Teams of 1-3 members will compete to complete the track in the fastest time.

Event Date

December 8, 2025

Participants

Classes 6-8 only

Team Size

2 members per team

Competition Rules

Robot Specifications

  • Maximum size: 25cm × 25cm × 25cm
  • Weight limit: 2kg
  • Must be fully autonomous
  • Basic sensors only for line detection

Track Details

  • White surface with black line
  • Line width: 2-3cm
  • Basic and sharp curves.

Scoring System

  • Time-based scoring only
  • Penalty for going off-track
  • Fastest time wins
