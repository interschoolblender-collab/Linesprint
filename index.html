About LineSprint' 25

A simple robotics competition where students in Classes 6-8 build autonomous robots to follow a black line on a white surface. Teams of 1-3 members will compete to complete the track in the fastest time.

Event Date

December 8, 2025

Participants

Classes 6-8 only

Team Size

2 members per team

Competition Rules

Robot Specifications

  • Maximum size: 25cm × 25cm × 25cm
  • Weight limit: 2kg
  • Must be fully autonomous
  • Must use Infrared (IR) sensors only
  • No external control (Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, Remote, GPS, Cloud)

Track Details

  • White surface with black line
  • Line width: 20mm - 30mm
  • Includes basic and sharp curves

Event Rules & Regulations

  • Each team may consist of a maximum of 2 students only
  • Only one team per house per category
  • Robots exceeding size limits will not participate
  • Report to venue at least 5 minutes early
  • No modifications allowed during rounds (except basic repairs)
  • Misconduct or unsafe behavior results in disqualification
  • Touching the robot during the run results in penalties
  • Judges’ decisions are final

Judgement Criteria

  • Accuracy
  • Stability
  • Build Quality
  • Speed
  • Precision
